Hurricane seasons

The Atlantic hurricane season is the period when hurricanes and tropical storms occur in the North Atlantic; it generally runs from June to November, with peak activity in September.

This page lists details of the recorded storms in the 2012 season. Storms are classified according to the Saffir-Simpson Hurricane Wind Scale based on sustained wind speed.
